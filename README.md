# Local Business Profit Planning

> **Domain:** Business Analysis

## Overview

Neighbourhood businesses often lack structured, data-backed planning. This project targeted nearby organisations to collect operational and sales data, analyse performance, and convert findings into clear actions. Pain points: scattered records, limited visibility into product/customer profitability, and intuition-led pricing and inventory decisions. Owners struggled to align promotions and stock with demand. Without intervention, margins would erode, cash would be tied up in slow-moving items, growth bets would remain guesswork, raising the risk of missed revenue targets and avoidable working-capital strain.

## Approach

- Engaged nearby organisations; mapped processes, objectives, constraints, decision cadence via structured interviews
- Consolidated ledgers, invoices, POS exports, surveys; cleaned and reconciled into single, analysis-ready dataset
- Assessed seasonality and cohorts; quantified product/customer profitability and drivers of margin leakage and stockouts
- Built planning model for pricing, promotions, reorder levels with simple what-if scenarios and playbooks
- Presented insights via brief reports and dashboards; set weekly reviews, owner approvals, action trackers
- Monitored outcomes and captured feedback; iterated recommendations after validating results against targets
